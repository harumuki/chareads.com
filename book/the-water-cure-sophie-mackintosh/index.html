Book sumary'Eerily beautiful, strange [and] unsettling' Paula Hawkins, author of The Girl on the Train 'Bewitching' Guardian 'Calmly devastating' Katherine Angel, author of Unmastered You are a girl. Your body is vulnerable. Men will break it if they can - and out there, they absolutely can. Suffering will prepare you for the worst. The cure is nothing compared to what you've been spared in the sickness. It takes a lot of love to hurt you like this. Now, come outside. It's time to play the drowning game. Imagine three sisters raised on an island, taught to fear the outside world and its men. And imagine the men who come to find them: three strangers washed up by the sea, bringing desire and destruction in their wake… 'A work of cool, claustrophobic beauty' Eli Goldstone, author of Strange Heart Beating 'Eerie, electric, beautiful. It rushes you through to the end on a tide of tension and closely held panic.
